The Problem

Philadelphia's tax code wasn't built for the modern workforce

The current system was designed for a W-2 economy. Today's independent workers are getting crushed.

A structural problem, not a rate problem

Philadelphia's two-class tax structure treats every non-corporation the same, whether you're a solo freelancer or a 50-person partnership.

This means individual business owners face double taxation on income already captured by the Net Profits Tax.

"We are taxing working families out of their homes."
The Solution

The LIFT Act

Create a third tax class for individuals and single-member LLCs, ending double taxation on true small businesses.
